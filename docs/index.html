How It Works

1

Speak Your Intent

Press Alt+S and speak naturally. "Show me all running docker containers" or "commit these changes with a message about fixing the auth bug."

2

AI Translates

Gemini Flash or local Parakeet model transcribes your voice and converts it into the appropriate CLI command with correct syntax and flags.

3

Execute Instantly

The command is automatically pasted into your active terminal and executed. Your hands never leave the keyboard.
